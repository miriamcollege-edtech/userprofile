About Me

Transforming education through strategic technology implementation and human-centered design

As a visionary strategist and tactical thinker in educational technology, I bring over 8 years of specialized experience in IT and EdTech, combining design thinking methodologies with deep technical expertise to transform the learning landscape at Miriam College.

My approach is rooted in Human-Centered Design, where the student experience takes center stage. I believe that technology should serve as an enabler—empowering educators and learners to achieve their fullest potential through thoughtfully designed digital ecosystems.

At Miriam College, I leverage my comprehensive background in UI/UX design, instructional design, and technical project management to drive strategic digital transformation initiatives that align with our institutional mission while preparing students for an increasingly digital future.
